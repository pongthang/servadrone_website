01 — About

Building India's
drone electronics future

India's drone industry has long relied on imported video transmission systems. Miliwatt Electronics is building indigenous high-performance digital video systems engineered for the demanding requirements of modern drone and robotic platforms.

Our mission is to establish India as a leader in mission-critical drone electronics — with ultra-low latency transmission, advanced RF engineering, and highly customizable OEM-ready platforms.
