01 — The Problem

Every team is either
locked in or rebuilding

Across inspection, defense, agriculture, logistics, and FPV — hundreds of drone and robotics companies are emerging with different architectures and mission requirements. Yet they all depend on the same critical foundation: low-latency, reliable real-time communication for video, control, and data.

That foundation is broken. Closed ecosystems like DJI and Walksnail deliver strong performance but enforce rigid architectures and platform lock-in. DIY solutions demand significant engineering effort to reach reliability — reinvented systems, delayed timelines, inconsistent results.

The result: enterprise OEMs cannot fully control their stack. Startups waste months rebuilding infrastructure instead of building product. FPV operators face high costs and poor modularity — a single crash often means replacing an entire system rather than a component.
