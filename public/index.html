01 — The Problem

rebuilding from scratch

Drone teams today choose between rigid, proprietary platforms that enforce vendor lock-in, or DIY stacks that drain months of engineering effort before a single flight. Neither path lets teams focus on what they actually build — autonomy, payload, or application-specific intelligence.

02 — IRIS

One system.
Every interface.

IRIS is a high-performance, modular drone communication system engineered for demanding OEM integration, defense, inspection, and FPV applications.
